About AI

We all live in a world filled with tasks which are boring and have to be done repeatedly and some other tasks like customer care etc. These tasks till about 30 years ago were completely done by humans! But now we have better technology and a new concept called A.I.

What is A.I or Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence (A.I) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think like humans and mimic their actions. Artificial Intelligence (A.I) is the branch of computer sciences that emphasizes the development of intelligent machines, thinking and working like humans. For example, speech recognition, problem-solving, learning and planning.

A bot made to learn using the power of A.I can be used in healthcare in the following manner: The bot has been given X-Rays for previous fractures of an arm of many different patients. These fractures can be classified into two parts total and partial half of the total fractures we have given to the bot in the form of illustrations, images and other forms of data are total fractures and half are partial fractures we have also taught the bot which fracture is partial and which one is total respectively, then we have trained it. this process takes a lot of time as it is classifying information and thousands of images. After it is trained, the bot is being used by the doctors they use it to analyze the type of fracture on the patient who is currently suffering from an arm fracture. But the doctors are not sure which type of fracture it is (total or partial). Thus they are not able to give the medication. The bot can compare the current fracture to the data and illustrations it was given during training. It will be able to make-out that which type of fracture is the current one. then the bot will give the information as output and grade it according to the data in the current X-Ray and the training data. This is way more efficient and in most cases more accurate. We can also program it to prescribe a medicine accordingly.

The bot was able to do this because of its learning capability this brings us to the branches of A.I
